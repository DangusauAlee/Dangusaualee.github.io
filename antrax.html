Methodology


1. Data Collection:
- Collected comprehensive data on their car sales, including information on models, brands, quantities sold, condition, and year of the vehicles.
- Internal databases, sales records, and transactional data was used as the primary data source.
2. Data Preprocessing:
- The collected data undergos a thorough preprocessing phase to ensure its quality and reliability.
- Any missing or erroneous values were addressed through appropriate imputation techniques or data cleansing procedures by either dropping or imputation.
3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):
- An initial exploratory analysis was conducted to get a better understanding of data to gain insights on centrality and variability.
- Statistical measures, visualizations, and summary statistics were employed to identify patterns, outliers, and distributions within the dataset.
- Relationships between variables was also tested using the EDA.
4. Feature Engineering:
- Based on the demand of the stakeholders to provide a revenue dashboard the approriate features were selected.
- Features that do not primarily shows revenue generation were dropped.
5. Data Visualization:
- The preprocessed data was imported to data visualization tool (Power BI) to create the revenue dashboard.
- The Visualization undergos an iterative process until all the features were captured.
- The revenue dashboard was then deployed and proper security and permissions were activated to give access to stakeholders as per roles of use
6. Decision-Making Framework:
- Antrax will develop a decision-making framework based on the insights derived from the dashboard.
- This framework prioritize revenue generation of car features to gain insights on customer preference.
- Antrax will use the framework to determine areas to maximize or rather pay attention to as they contribute effectively to revenue generation.
7. Sensitivity Analysis:
- Sensitivity analysis will be conducted to assess the robustness of the decision-making framework.
- Various scenarios will be simulated to gauge the framework's performance under different conditions.
- This analysis will provide Antrax with a better understanding of the potential risks and uncertainties associated with their strategic decisions.
8. Reporting and Recommendations:
- The findings, insights, and recommendations resulting from the data analysis project will be documented in a comprehensive report.
- The report will include a detailed explanation of the methodology employed, the key findings, and the recommended strategy for Antrax to monitor their revenue.

Revenue Analysis

Insights

Revenue Overview: Achieving Exemplary Success

Over the years, Antrax Ventures demonstrated exceptional performance, cumilating in a total revenue of 12.09 billion naira from the sales of 1444 vehicles of 199 models from 40 manufacturers. This remarkable achievement is a testament of their collective efforts and unwavering commitment to delivering exemplary services to their valued customers.

Engine Type Impact: Optimizing Efficiency and Demand

An in-depth revenue analysis based on engine types has unveiled compelling observations. 4-cylinder (I4) engines emerged as the primary revenue drivers, accounting for an impressive 6.4 billion naira. This emphasizes the increasing demand for fuel-efficient and practical vehicles in their extensive inventory. Leveraging this knowledge, Antrax Ventures can strategically optimize offerings to cater to customer preferences effectively.

Body Paint Preferences: A Palette for Success

An exploration of customer body paint preferences has yielded intriguing insights. Black-colored vehicles commanded the highest demand, generating a substantial 5.3 billion naira in revenue. Following closely, White and Gray also made noteworthy contributions with 1.8 billion naira and 1.5 billion naira, respectively. Armed with this knowledge, Antrax Ventures can align inventory to capitalize on customer preferences and maximize revenue potential.

Transmission and Fuel Type Insights: Fuelling Revenue Growth

Understanding customer preferences concerning transmission and fuel types is vital to the business success. Our data-driven analysis has revealed that vehicles equipped with automatic transmission and petrol engines were the key revenue drivers, contributing significantly to the total revenue of 12 billion naira. By catering to these preferences, Antrax Ventures can enhance customer satisfaction and foster sustainable revenue growth.

Condition Matters: Decoding Customer Choices

An assessment of revenue by condition has shed light on customer preferences. Foreign used cars emerged as the dominant revenue generators, contributing 9 billion naira, constituting 75.8% of the total revenue. Additionally, new cars also made substantial contributions, accounting for 3 billion naira, constituting 24.2% of the total. Acknowledging and aligning with these preferences will empower Antrax Ventures to effectively cater to diverse customer segments.

Location Matters: Navigating Revenue Hotspots

The impact of location on revenue has been a pivotal finding. Lagos, being a bustling city, emerged as the primary revenue hotspot, with foreign used cars in Lagos commanding the largest portion of 8 billion naira. Abuja followed suit, further emphasizing the significance of these markets. By capitalizing on regional preferences and demand, Antrax Ventures can optimize revenue opportunities in each location.

Odometer and Price Relationship: Unveiling Customer Perception

The relationship between average odometer readings and vehicle prices is a noteworthy revelation. As the data suggests, there is a negative correlation between the two, indicating that vehicles with higher odometer readings tend to have lower prices. Moreover, the data underscores that new cars generally command higher prices than foreign used vehicles. This insight will enable Antrax Ventures to better understand customer value perception and adjust pricing strategies accordingly.

Manufacturing Year Impact: Aligning with Market Trends

Analyzing the impact of manufacturing years on revenue has provided valuable market insights. Cars manufactured after 2010 exhibited a decline in average odometer readings, making them more appealing to customers. Additionally, newer vehicles commanded higher prices, indicating a growing preference for recent models with lower mileage. By staying attuned to these trends, Antrax Ventures can align inventory to resonate with evolving customer expectations.

Top Revenue-Generating Models: Leveraging Customer Preferences

Our analysis of revenue by model has spotlighted the top-performing models in our inventory. LX, Camry, and Rover emerged as the most lucrative models, each contributing substantially to the total revenue, with LX leading the pack at 1.17 billion naira. Understanding these customer preferences will empower Antrax Ventures to strategically promote and expand offerings aligned with these popular models.

Manufacturer Influence: Strengthening Partnerships

Revenue analysis by manufacturer has emphasized the significance of brand recognition. Toyota, Lexus, and Benz have emerged as leading manufacturers, contributing substantially to Antrax Ventures' success. Toyota, in particular, generated an impressive 4.32 billion naira in revenue. Strengthening partnerships with these reputable manufacturers can bolster market presence and customer appeal.

In conclusion, the revenue dashboard analysis has furnished Antrax Ventures with invaluable insights to inform strategic decisions, elevate customer satisfaction, and drive sustained growth. As we embark on this data-driven journey, rest assured that Antrax will leverage these findings to capitalize on opportunities, address challenges, and deliver exceptional experiences to our esteemed customers.

Recommendations

Based on the insights gleaned from the revenue analysis, here are comprehensive recommendations for Antrax Ventures to capitalize on opportunities and enhance business performance:

Focus on Popular Engine Types

Given that 4-cylinder (I4) engines are the primary revenue drivers, Antrax Ventures should continue to prioritize vehicles with this engine type in its inventory. Additionally, market research and customer feedback can identify emerging trends in engine preferences to adapt offerings accordingly.

Strategically Stock Preferred Body Paints

With Black, White, and Gray being the most popular body paint colors, Antrax Ventures should ensure a sufficient stock of vehicles painted in these colors. Moreover, the company can explore offering customizable paint options to cater to customers' unique preferences.

Leverage Automatic Transmission and Petrol Engines

As these transmission and fuel types significantly impact revenue, Antrax Ventures should expand its offerings with a broad selection of vehicles featuring automatic transmission and petrol engines. Customer education about the benefits of these options can also strengthen demand.

Enhance Foreign Used Car Offerings

Foreign used cars are a key revenue driver, representing the majority of sales. To further capitalize on this demand, Antrax Ventures can source high-quality, well-maintained foreign used vehicles and offer attractive financing options to appeal to a broader customer base.

Optimize Market Presence in High-Revenue Locations

Lagos and Abuja have proven to be lucrative markets for Antrax Ventures. The company should invest in targeted marketing campaigns, dealership expansions, and customer engagement activities to solidify its presence in these cities and maintain a competitive edge.

Differentiate with New Car Offerings

While foreign used cars lead in revenue, Antrax Ventures should leverage its relationships with reputable manufacturers to expand and promote its new car offerings. Demonstrating the benefits of purchasing new vehicles, such as warranty coverage and the latest technology, can attract customers seeking premium options.

Price Odometer-Price Strategically

Utilizing the negative correlation between odometer readings and vehicle prices, Antrax Ventures can create a transparent pricing strategy that appeals to customers seeking budget-friendly options. Additionally, the company can highlight the value proposition of newer vehicles with higher prices.

Align Inventory with Market Trends

As manufacturing years impact customer preferences, Antrax Ventures should align its inventory to reflect market trends. Prioritize newer models with lower odometer readings to cater to the growing demand for such vehicles.

Promote Top Revenue-Generating Models

LX, Camry, and Rover have demonstrated significant revenue potential. Antrax Ventures can invest in targeted marketing campaigns, exclusive offers, and incentives to drive further sales for these popular models.

Strengthen Manufacturer Partnerships

Building strong partnerships with leading manufacturers like Toyota, Lexus, and Benz is essential for continued success. Antrax Ventures should collaborate closely with these brands to gain exclusive deals, early access to new models, and enhanced support.

Embrace Data-Driven Decision-Making

As a data analyst, continuing to adopt data-driven decision-making is paramount for Antrax Ventures' success. Regularly analyze revenue data, customer feedback, and market trends to identify emerging opportunities and challenges.

Invest in Customer Experience

Delivering exceptional customer experiences is key to building brand loyalty and fostering repeat business. Antrax Ventures should focus on providing excellent customer service, transparent sales processes, and personalized after-sales support.

By implementing these comprehensive recommendations, Antrax Ventures can position itself for sustained growth, competitive advantage, and increased customer satisfaction in the dynamic car retail industry.
